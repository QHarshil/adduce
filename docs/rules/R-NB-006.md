# R-NB-006 — Seeding precedes randomness in notebooks

**Category:** Notebooks  
**Weight:** 2

## Why it matters

A notebook that draws random numbers before (or without) seeding differs on every rerun.

## Suppressing

Inline, on the reported line: `# adduce: ignore=R-NB-006`  
Project-wide, in `adduce.toml` or `[tool.adduce]`: `ignore = ["R-NB-006"]`

Suppressed findings still appear in reports, marked as ignored.
